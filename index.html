Coinage

What to know, January 14, 2025

For the extent of recorded history exchange has taken place using either basic bartering or via the use of something called coinage. Coins are essentially tokens of arbitrary material and size which are assigned value by a governing body. They have been used for thousands of years, continuing on to this very day, some notable contempory examples of coinage are, the cent, the euro, the marc, the yen, etc.

Bills

A brief history, January 14, 2025

Although more recent, these pieces of paper have been proliferated to nearly all global and local economies, they abstractly represent value in the same manner as coins, but can be more cheaply produced and are easier to scale. These notes have been the choice of majority first world country and plethora small jurisdictions.
